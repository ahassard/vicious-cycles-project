Who are we?

Vicious Cycles Inc. is a Triangle area motorcycle shop founded by a few like-minded individuals in the late 2010's. Our mechanics build the bikes and man the workshops. Our customer service staff tends to the day to day operations putting our customers first. We hope to create and foster a strong passion for the local bike scene because each and every one of us is a motorcycle enthusiast.

We Got You!

Vicious cyclesoffers a variety of motorcycle and some Trikes as well, from Honda, Suzuki, Kawasaki,Yamaha, Triumph, and more, we even have the ability to custom build the bike of yourdreams.

Services

We have service centers that can take care of maintenance, repairs, and even a nice paint job. Not only can we take care of your new bike, we can get you outfitted withthe right gear, apparel and accessories.

Our Mission Statement

Owning a motorcycle and knowing the open road is a lifestyle, it chooses you, youdon’t choose it. We strive to make the open road the way you invision it!

We Have The Best Customers!

We don’t just customize bikes. We also sell stuff we know you’ll dig. Riding is a lifestyle,and we want to help you live your life.
